Institute Level Events

Events

1. Group Dance (चांस पे डांस)

Rules & Regulations:
1. One entry per college.
2. Time limit is 08-10 minutes for each performance. Negative marking for exceeding the time limit.
3. Each team may have 8-20 members, including coordinators, Makeup Artists, Choreographers etc.
4. Choice/genre of songs is open to the participants. But, vulgarity & obscenity at any point are strictly not allowed and can lead to immediate disqualification.
5. Any kind of harm to the auditorium or stage is strictly not allowed. Lighting a fire, cigarette or using water or alcohol is strictly not allowed in Institution premises.
6. No props will be provided by the coordinators. Participants can carry their own props.
7. Green room would be given for changing purpose.
8. The participants will be judged on the basis of their rhythm, formation, synchronization, expressions, and costumes, makeup, setup and overall effects.
9. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
10. Prizes:
   • Winner: 11,000
   • 1st Runner Up: 7000
   • 2nd Runner Up: Trophy & Certificate

2. Group Singing (रागिनी)

Rules & Regulations:
1. One entry per college.
2. Time limit is 08-10 minutes including sound track. Negative marking for exceeding the time limit.
3. Each team may have 04-15 members, including coordinators, Musicians, Lyricist etc.
4. The participants will have to carry their own instruments. Maximum of 3 instruments are allowed.
5. Choice/genre of songs is open to the participants. But, any song which has slang or causes any harm to religion, community, cast, creed, society, etc. would be disqualified immediately.
6. Any kind of harm to the auditorium or stage is strictly not allowed. Lighting a fire, cigarette or using water or alcohol is strictly not allowed in Institution premises.
7. No props will be provided by the coordinators. Participants can carry their own props.
8. Participants will not be allowed to refer to the lyrics while singing.
9. The participants will be judged on the basis of their technique, expression, rhythm/ timing, choreography, costumes, makeup, setup and overall effects.
10. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
11. Prizes:
    • Winner: 11,000
    • 1st Runner Up: 7000
    • 2nd Runner Up: Trophy & Certificate
